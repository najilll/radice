Osborne 1 — The First Portable Computer

Step back into 1981 and explore the Osborne 1 — the world’s first commercially successful portable computer. Experience a 3D view of the machine that started the mobile computing revolution.

Released in 1981, the Osborne 1 was a groundbreaking step in computer mobility. Weighing about 10.7 kg (24.5 lbs) and featuring a 5-inch display, dual floppy drives, and bundled software, it allowed professionals to carry computing power wherever they went. Though bulky by modern standards, the Osborne 1 paved the way for laptops, setting a Academy for the portable and connected world we know today.
