Our Focus

Radice Foundation is dedicated to empowering learners with knowledge, skills, and opportunities. Discover our Vision, Mission, and Values below, guiding every step of our journey.

At Radice Foundation, our vision is the driving force behind everything we do. We aspire to redefine the landscape of online education by creating opportunities that are accessible to everyone, regardless of background or location.

  • Accessible and high-quality learning experiences for all age groups.
  • Practical and industry-relevant knowledge to bridge the skills gap.
  • Empowerment of students and professionals to achieve personal and career growth.
  • Innovative learning tools and platforms that adapt to the evolving needs of learners.
  • A community-driven environment that promotes collaboration and lifelong learning.

Our mission defines our actions. Radice Foundation is committed to creating impactful learning experiences that not only impart knowledge but also develop skills, confidence, and career readiness for all learners.

  • Deliver innovative and practical online learning programs that are easy to access and understand.
  • Combine expert guidance with modern technology to enhance the learning experience.
  • Ensure learners gain the knowledge, skills, and confidence to succeed in competitive industries.
  • Foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving through interactive courses.
  • Continuously adapt our programs based on learner feedback and emerging trends in education.

Values are the heart of our organization. They guide our decisions, shape our culture, and define how we interact with learners, partners, and the community at large. Radice Foundation is built on principles that inspire trust, dedication, and excellence.

  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all interactions.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in our courses, support, and outcomes.
  • Innovation: Continuously exploring new ideas, methods, and technologies to improve learning.
  • Collaboration: Promoting teamwork and partnerships to create a stronger learning community.
  • Empowerment: Enabling learners to take charge of their personal and professional growth.
  • Inclusivity: Providing equal opportunities for learners from all backgrounds.

Osborne 1 — The First Portable Computer

Step back into 1981 and explore the Osborne 1 — the world’s first commercially successful portable computer. Experience a 3D view of the machine that started the mobile computing revolution.

Released in 1981, the Osborne 1 was a groundbreaking step in computer mobility. Weighing about 10.7 kg (24.5 lbs) and featuring a 5-inch display, dual floppy drives, and bundled software, it allowed professionals to carry computing power wherever they went. Though bulky by modern standards, the Osborne 1 paved the way for laptops, setting a foundation for the portable and connected world we know today.
